How They Fit

One product family for recurring public evidence problems.

Nexus Social Recon handles visible identity and platform evidence. Lens Witness focuses on phone camera, phone screen, and device gallery evidence from the computer.

Product Primary Evidence Core Output Best Fit
Nexus Social Recon Visible public social profiles, posts, comments, media, account links, and recurring identifiers selected by the user. HTML/PDF reports, media folders, OCR, hashes, bookmarks, User Index links, local cross-case lookup, and case intelligence. Investigations where online identity, posts, relationships, or social media artifacts matter.
Lens Witness Mobile screen recordings, phone camera recordings, snapshots, phone gallery imports, and captured visual context. Public recording projects or forensic case packages with reports, audit logs, SHA-256 hashes, timestamps, and review context. Public users who need clean phone recording, and agencies that need mobile capture tied to case documentation.
