How They Fit

One product family for recurring public evidence problems.

Nexus Social Recon handles identity and platform evidence. Video Anchor focuses on moments inside moving media. Lens Witness focuses on phone camera, phone screen, and device gallery evidence from the computer.

Product Primary Evidence Core Output Best Fit
Nexus Social Recon Public social profiles, posts, comments, media, account links, and recurring identifiers. HTML/PDF reports, media folders, OCR, hashes, bookmarks, and case intelligence. Investigations where online identity, posts, relationships, or social media artifacts matter.
Video Anchor Long videos, public video sources, saved case media, clips, frames, and time-coded notes. Anchored video timelines, clip packages, stills, transcripts, source context, or Pro forensic reports with audit logs and SHA-256 hash manifests. Public users who need clean video review, and agencies that need video findings tied to case documentation.
Lens Witness Mobile screen recordings, phone camera recordings, snapshots, phone gallery imports, and captured visual context. Public recording projects or forensic case packages with reports, audit logs, SHA-256 hashes, timestamps, and review context. Public users who need clean phone recording, and agencies that need mobile capture tied to case documentation.
