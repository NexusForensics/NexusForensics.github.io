How They Fit

One product family for recurring public evidence problems.

Nexus Social Recon handles identity and platform evidence. Video Anchor focuses on moments inside moving media. Lens Witness focuses on recording mobile screen or mobile camera evidence from the computer.

Product Primary Evidence Core Output Best Fit
Nexus Social Recon Public social profiles, posts, comments, media, account links, and recurring identifiers. HTML/PDF reports, media folders, OCR, hashes, bookmarks, and case intelligence. Investigations where online identity, posts, relationships, or social media artifacts matter.
Video Anchor Long videos, public video sources, saved case media, clips, frames, and time-coded notes. Anchored video timelines, clip packages, stills, transcripts, source context, and hash manifests. Investigations where the important evidence is a moment inside a larger video record.
Lens Witness Mobile screen recordings, phone camera recordings, screenshots, source notes, and captured visual context. Computer-recorded mobile evidence packages with notes, timestamps, hashes, and review context. Investigations where phone activity or live phone-camera views need to be recorded and explained clearly.
