Mobile Witness Recording

Lens Witness

A Nexus Forensics product for recording mobile phone evidence on the computer. Capture what is happening on the phone screen, or record the phone camera feed, then keep the recording reviewable with notes, timestamps, and case context.

Two recording modes See workflow Contact sales

What It Does

Record mobile phone evidence on the computer.

Lens Witness is built around two practical capture modes: recording the phone screen as it appears, and recording the phone camera feed through the computer for reviewable witness material.

Record what is on the mobile phone

Mirror or connect the phone to the computer and record the screen activity as evidence. The recording can preserve app activity, visible messages, pages, settings, or other phone-screen context.

Record the phone camera on the computer

Use the phone camera as the live visual source and record that feed on the computer. This supports scene walkthroughs, object views, documents, surroundings, and other camera-based witness evidence.

Workflow

From phone connection to witness-ready recording.

The workflow keeps the operator focused on the phone source, the recording mode, and the case context needed to explain the capture later.

Step 1Connect phone

Attach or mirror the mobile device to the investigator computer.

Step 2Choose mode

Select phone-screen recording or phone-camera recording.

Step 3Record

Capture the phone activity or camera feed with timestamps and case context.

Step 4Review

Add notes, mark relevant moments, and preserve local recording details.

Step 5Package

Export the recording, notes, hashes, and review context for the case file.

Recording Examples

Show the phone source on the computer while recording it.

Lens Witness records phone evidence as the operator sees it. Use live phone mirroring for screen activity, or switch to live phone camera recording when the evidence is what the phone camera is pointed at.
