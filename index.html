Digital Evidence Software

Nexus Forensics

Nexus Forensics develops secure, professional software for law enforcement agencies, corporate legal teams, and licensed private investigators. Across our product family, we help professionals capture, preserve, organize, review, and report digital evidence in structured and defensible ways.

Our work is guided by three principles: transparency in how evidence is handled and presented; compliance-conscious workflows that respect applicable laws, policies, privacy requirements, and platform terms; and efficiency that helps investigators move from collection to clear, professional reporting with less friction.

Whether the source is public online content, witness media, or other investigator-selected material, Nexus Forensics keeps the user in control while preserving context, provenance, and accountability from capture through report.
