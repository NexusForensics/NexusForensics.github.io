How Nexus Is Different

Nexus Social Recon is built for investigators who need to preserve visible public social evidence, decide what matters, and keep every finding connected to its source. It brings real-time on-screen capture, structured case work, local analysis, and professional reporting into one investigator-controlled workflow.

The Nexus Approach

Capture is only the beginning.

Nexus Social Recon is designed around the complete investigative path from seeing public content to producing an organized, source-linked case record.

Capture what is visible

Preserve investigator-selected public content as it appears on screen, with screenshots, source URLs, visible comments, and local files kept close to the case.

Review what matters

Use OCR, keyword search, bookmarks, and visual comparison to move from collected material to findings that deserve attention.

Connect the evidence

Compare repeated identifiers, User Index links, media details, and local cross-case signals without separating the lead from its supporting evidence.

Show the source trail

Create readable reports with thumbnails, links, notes, hashes, manifests, and local evidence paths that help reviewers understand each finding.

Investigator Workflow

Built around decisions an investigator actually makes.

The workflow stays understandable from the first public view to the final report.

Step 1See it

Review visible public content in real time through the standard platform view.

Step 2Capture it

Preserve selected screenshots, visible details, source URLs, and local evidence.

Step 3Review it

Use OCR, keywords, bookmarks, and visual comparison to identify relevant material.

Step 4Connect it

Compare identifiers, User Index links, media details, and local case signals.

Step 5Report it

Produce source-linked reports with notes, thumbnails, hashes, and manifests.

Design Principles

Investigator control stays visible.

Nexus is designed to make the handling, analysis, and presentation of public evidence easier to understand—not more opaque.

Local by default

Core case files and analysis can remain on the investigator's workstation or organization-controlled storage. Cloud processing is not required.

Reviewable intelligence

Potential matches are presented with supporting thumbnails, source context, links, and confidence information so the investigator can assess the result.

Compliance-conscious capture

Visible, user-directed workflows keep evidence collection focused on what the authorized investigator sees, selects, and preserves during review.

A Focused Evidence Layer

Designed to complement the rest of the toolkit.

Nexus Social Recon is not presented as a replacement for every archive, device-forensic suite, or enterprise intelligence platform. It addresses the point where live public content must become organized case evidence.

Alongside forensic labs

Preserve relevant public evidence that may change or disappear before a device becomes available for forensic examination.

Alongside archives

Investigate user-selected public sources while organizational archives continue handling continuous records retention and compliance.

Alongside OSINT platforms

Preserve, organize, and report the visible evidence that supports a lead discovered through broader intelligence work.
